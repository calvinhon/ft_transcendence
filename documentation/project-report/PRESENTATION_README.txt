PRESENTATION TIPS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Time Allocation:
  • Introduction & Requirements: 10 minutes
  • Technical & Architecture: 15 minutes
  • Team Contributions: 12 minutes (2 min per member)
  • Live Demonstration: 15 minutes
  • Q&A Discussion: 15 minutes
  • Buffer: 5 minutes
  Total: ~60 minutes

Tips for Effective Presentation:
  1. Print presentation on PDF for reference notes
  2. Have a backup copy on USB drive
  3. Test projector/display connection before presentation
  4. Prepare live demo environment (Docker containers running)
  5. Assign speaking points to each team member
  6. Practice transitions between sections
  7. Be ready to deep-dive into any technical section
  8. Keep eye contact with audience
  9. Speak clearly and at moderate pace
  10. Acknowledge questions and provide thorough answers

Handling Questions:
  • Questions can be raised at any time
  • Refer to specific slide numbers when explaining
  • Use live demonstration to prove functionality
  • For unknown questions: "Let me investigate and get back to you"
  • Have source code open for code questions
